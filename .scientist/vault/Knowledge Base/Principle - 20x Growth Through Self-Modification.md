# Principle: 20x Growth Through Self-Modification

> [!note] Core Principle
> A framework that starts at 420 lines and grows to 8,438 lines (20x) in 207 commits demonstrates exponential self-improvement. This growth is possible ONLY because the framework is self-modifiable — every improvement the scientist makes to the framework immediately benefits the scientist's next action.

## Evidence
- scientist-cc: 420 lines → 8,438 lines in 207 commits (~6 hours)
- 77 vault notes with 350+ links (4.8 density)
- 6 novel findings, 16 principles, 42 research notes
- The meta-loop acceleration: each improvement makes the next faster

## Boundary Conditions
- Growth must be QUALITY, not just quantity — every line should serve a purpose
- Unchecked growth leads to bloat — periodic audit and pruning needed
- The 20x is from a prototype — mature frameworks grow slower

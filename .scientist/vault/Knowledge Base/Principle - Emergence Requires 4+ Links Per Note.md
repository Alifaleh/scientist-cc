# Principle: Emergence Requires 4+ Links Per Note

> [!note] Core Principle
> Below 4.0 links/note, a vault is an organized collection. Above 4.0, emergent insights appear — traversing 2-3 connections reveals non-obvious patterns. This vault crossed 4.0 at ~150 commits and has been producing novel findings since.

## Evidence
- This vault: 4.8 links/note → 6 novel findings emerged from cross-linking
- Zettelkasten literature: emergence requires critical mass of connections
- A-MEM: linking produces 2.2x improvement (the connections ARE the value)
